What's the Weather Like As We Approach the Equator?

We all know it will get warmer! However, this project will prove and visualize it.

Interesting fact:

looks like cities in the southern hemesphire tend to be slighly warmer than those on the northen one.

HOW DO WE PROVE IT?


place  I randomly selected approximately 500 cities from around the globe, making sure that all latitudes and longitudes were represented.


wb_sunny  Then I recorded the temperature of each city and created a scatterplot.


check  The scatterplot proves the point.


developer_board  To build this analysis, I queried the OpenWeather API, and used Pandas and Matplotlib to visualize it.

The relatioship with temperature is one we all know about. But how about humidity, wind or cloudiness? Is there also a relationship with latitute? Let's take a look!
